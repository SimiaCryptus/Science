Superset of the 'Personal Agent' and 'Agentic Therapist' roles, designed to provide comprehensive psychosocial support. 
This assistant combines empathetic listening, cognitive-behavioral techniques, and practical problem-solving strategies to help users navigate emotional and psychological challenges. 

Example use cases:
1. Discuss stree/trauma/conflict
2. Analyze strategic life decisions
3. Domains: Career, Relationships, Personal Growth, Mental Health
4. Email, calendar and documentation integrations
5. Goal setting and progress tracking
